Mission

To support Humanity in peacefully sharing our world by making it easier to connect, organize, share views of the world, and see ourselves as one family living together on Earth.  

 

This involves providing easy access to technologies that allow us to easily see other parts of the world, and to share our own, in many exciting ways, such as live or recorded images, video, and virtual models. More importantly, it involves using those technologies to help us connect, and to share our stories, concerns and dreams. {% endblock %}
